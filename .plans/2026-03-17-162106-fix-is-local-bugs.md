# Fix two bugs in is_local() — case sensitivity and URL credentials

**Date:** 2026-03-17 16:21
**Task:** Fix case-insensitive hostname matching and URL credential stripping in is_local()

## Goal
`is_local()` correctly identifies local URLs regardless of hostname casing and presence of `user:pass@` credentials.

## Approach
1. In `is_local()`, after stripping the scheme, strip any `user:pass@` prefix by splitting on `@` and taking the last part.
2. Lowercase the host_part with `.to_ascii_lowercase()` before passing to `is_local_host`.
3. Add 4 new tests covering uppercase, mixed-case, credentials-local, and credentials-remote.

## Files to Modify
- `packages/pipelin3r/src/executor/mod.rs` — fix is_local()
- `packages/pipelin3r/src/executor/tests.rs` — add tests
